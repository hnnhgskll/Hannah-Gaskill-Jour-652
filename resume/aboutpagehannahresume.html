Hannah Gaskill is a mixed media artist and journalist. Originally from Ocean City, MD, Hannah moved to Pittsburgh, PA, to pursue a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from Carnegie Mellon University's College of Fine Arts. Hannah has had the pleasure of showing work at numerous artistic institutions around the Pittsburgh region, notably the Mattress Factory Installation Art Museum and Bunker Projects. In August of 2018, Gaskill moved to College Park, MD, to pursue a Masters of Journalism degree. She is interested in broadcast production, civil literacy, and public news comprehension and accessibility.
